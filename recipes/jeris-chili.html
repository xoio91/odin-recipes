Jeri's Chili

Description

A delicious, cold weather staple whose recipe has been passed around the entire eastern United States. Perfected by the one and only Jeri Erickson, adapted from the lesser known Giada De Laurentiis.

This is a simple dish to prepare, and it makes for a great weekday meal as is easy to freeze and reheat. Fan favorite pairings include a fresh baguette, Tostitos Scoops, or cornbread.
